About Me


I am deeply passionate about game development and proficient in both game programming and 3D artistry. With a solid grasp of Computer Science, Unity, and Blender, I am currently immersed in the realm of what is now my 3rd Year in Game Application Development at Abertay Univeristy.

My Passion

I absolutely love the fusion of art, programming, and storytelling that game development offers. With hands-on experience in programming, 3D modeling, and game mechanics design, I've honed my skills through various projects, including creating game worlds, managing projects via Git Bash, team management, gameplay programming, asset creation and many more...
My key strengths lie in my ability to blend my creativity with my technical skills, ensuring that each aspect of the game can thrive harmoniously.

My Goals and Aspirations

My ultimate goal in my career is to craft emotionally captivating and innovative games that can push the limits of storytelling and gameplay. I aspire to specialize in creating consequential gameplay that delve deep into the human experience so that everything the player does will have an impact on their future. I wish to use and take advantage of AI and other upcoming technology for these immersive experiences across multiple platforms, including PC, console, and virtual reality to show the full potential of games.

Extracurricular Achievements

In addition to my academic learning, I have actively pursued and participated in GameJams to get used to working in different sized teams and environments. While it has proven difficult on occasions, it has never stopped me from bringing out the best in the teams I managed and the projects I have worked on. My dedication to allocating the correct jobs to the right people and that ensuring everything runs smoothly has proven to be absolutely essential and has allowed the team and I to not only keep our Git repository conflicts low but to also produce working games in such a limited amount of time.
